  • Combustion Theory by F. A. Williams, ABP (FAW)
  • Understanding Combustion by H. S. Mukunda, Macmillan India (HSM)
  • Combustion Physics by C. K. Law (CKL)
  • Fuels and Combustion, Smith and Stinson, McGraw Hill, New York, 1952
  • Combustion and Gasification in Fluidized Beds, Taylor & Francis Group, LLC, 2006
  • Combustion Technology: Essentials of Flames and Burners, by V. Raghavan, Ane/Athena Books, 2016 (VR)
  • Theoretical and Numerical Combustion by Thierry Poinsot and Denis Veynante, 2016 (TP)
